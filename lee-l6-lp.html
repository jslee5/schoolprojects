About

Big K.R.I.T. returns with the Digital Rose Tour, an uncannily loose yet precise rapper and accomplished producer, Big K.R.I.T. has continued in a lineage of Southern rap legends who include UGK, 8Ball and MJG, OutKast, and David Banner.

He spent years honing his skills on the mixtape circuit before signing with Def Jam, a deal that led to the Top Ten albums Live from the Underground and Cadillactica.

K.R.I.T. has since been independent with his Multi Alumni label, the outlet for his third consecutive Top Ten full-length, 4eva Is a Mighty Long Time, and a set of later EPs summarized as TDT.

He has either produced and appeared on material from the likes of Wiz Khalifa, Curren$y, Freddie Gibbs, and Rick Ross.
